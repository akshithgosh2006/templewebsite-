Legend & Origin

The Kozhikodankandi Sree Bhagavathi Temple holds a rich legacy deeply rooted in the cultural fabric of Kerala. According to the ancient legends, the divine presence of Sree Bhagavathi graced this sacred land to protect the devotees and vanquish evil.

Passed down through generations, the traditions observed here are a testament to our steadfast faith. The temple architecture, the serene pond, and the majestic banyan tree have witnessed centuries of devotion, rituals, and grand festivals.
